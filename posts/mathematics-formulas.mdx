# Mathematics and Formulas in Computer Science

### Linear Algebra

#### Matrices and Determinants

- **Matrix Multiplication**: $$(AB)_{ij} = \sum_{k=1}^{n} A_{ik}B_{kj}$$

- **2x2 Determinant**: $$\begin{vmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{vmatrix} = ad - bc$$

#### Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors

For a matrix $A$, a non-zero vector $\mathbf{v}$ is an eigenvector if:
$$A\mathbf{v} = \lambda\mathbf{v}$$

### Calculus

#### Basic Derivatives

- $$\frac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1}$$
- $$\frac{d}{dx}e^x = e^x$$
- $$\frac{d}{dx}\ln(x) = \frac{1}{x}$$

#### Integrals

- $$\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \quad (n \neq -1)$$
- $$\int e^x dx = e^x + C$$
- $$\int \frac{1}{x} dx = \ln|x| + C$$

### Probability and Statistics

#### Probability Formulas

- **Conditional Probability**: $$P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}$$
- **Bayes' Theorem**: $$P(A|B) = \frac{P(B|A)P(A)}{P(B)}$$

#### Probability Distributions

- **Normal Distribution**:
  $$f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}\right)^2}$$

### Graph Theory

#### Euler's Formula for Planar Graphs

$$V - E + F = 2$$

Where:

- $V$: number of vertices
- $E$: number of edges
- $F$: number of faces

### Cryptography

#### RSA Encryption

1. Choose two large prime numbers $p$ and $q$
2. Calculate $n = p \times q$
3. Calculate $\phi(n) = (p-1)(q-1)$
4. Choose $e$ such that $1 < e < \phi(n)$ and $\gcd(e, \phi(n)) = 1$
5. Find $d$ such that $d \times e \equiv 1 \pmod{\phi(n)}$

Public key: $(e, n)$ Private key: $(d, n)$

### Artificial Intelligence

#### Sigmoid Activation Function

$$\sigma(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-x}}$$

#### Cross-Entropy Loss Function

$$L = -\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N} [y_i \log(\hat{y}_i) + (1-y_i)\log(1-\hat{y}_i)]$$

### Conclusion

Mathematical formulas form the foundation of computer science and programming. Understanding these
concepts is crucial for developing algorithms and solving complex problems in the field.
